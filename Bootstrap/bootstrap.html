About

Belgium is known for four (4) things: fries, waffles, beer, and chocolate.

There are three (3) regions in Belgium: French Region or Wallonia, Flemish Region or Flanders, and Brussels-Capital Region.

Belgians speak three (3) different languages: French, Dutch, and German.
